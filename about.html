ABOUT US

Pine City Mall is the ultimate fashion, entertainment and dining destination in Durban.
With over 15+ stores, a host of exciting entertainment and visitors are spoilt for choice!
At Pine City you will find local South African retail, popular international brands such
as H and M, Apple and everything in between. Pine City also features an 18 cinema Ster Kinekor
complex, IMAX Theatre, Funtubbles Family Funfair, Electric Avenue bowling, Action Karting,
be.UP Park and much more!


Pine City Mall is headed by Excellerate Real Estate Services. Excellerate Real Estate
Services (Pty) Ltd t/a JHI, is a trusted property services company that delivers, comprehensive
strategies, services, and solutions to a wide client base. Prepare your senses for a world class experience at one of the largest shopping centres in the
Southern Hemisphere.
