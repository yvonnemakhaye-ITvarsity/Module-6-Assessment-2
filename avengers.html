Movie synopsis

Thanos snapped his fingers in Avengers: Infinity War, and half the life in the universe instantly dissolved. Now a ragged group of surviving warriors, including Captain America,
Black Widow, Iron Man, Thor, and the Hulk works to counteract the Mad Titan’s genocidal action. Since the heroes could not stop Thanos the first time, what makes them think
they can win in a rematch? The answer may be in overwhelming power, thanks to Captain Marvel, or in the tiniest spaces between atoms accessible to Ant-Man. Avengers: Endgame
will rewrite the Marvel Cinematic Universe as we know it. When it’s all over, nothing will be the same.
